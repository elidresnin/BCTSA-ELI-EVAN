About

Mega Escape opened in 2014, in our King of Prussia location. Business was small at first, but started to increase steadily. Suddenly, completely by chance, we won Best Escape Room in the King of Prussia area, by the King of Prussia Magazine. Many awards followed. Busiess skyrocketed, and we were able to open our Cherry Hill Location in November 2015. Each location now has 3 escape rooms. In August of 2016, we opened our newest location in Wilmington, Delaware. We offer some of the best escape rooms in the tri-state area. Only the best for the life-changing expeirence that we have at Mega Escape!
